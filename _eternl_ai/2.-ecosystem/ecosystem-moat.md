# Ecosystem Moat

Eternl.ai aims to [scale our Game Hub](scaling-of-game-hub.md) within a year, establishing a robust moat and virtuous growth cycle enabled by the deeply integrated ecosystem.

As our ecosystem has more games, it has a: &#x20;

* **More Omnipotent Game AGI**: compatible with more games across genres and gameplay design.&#x20;
* **More Retentive Game Hub:** providing a larger platform for AI assets and social network to flourish.&#x20;
* **More Cost-effective Computing Network:** offering more reliable and cost-efficient inferencing.&#x20;

As our ecosystem has more data, it has a:&#x20;

* **More Powerful Gaming AG**I: improved by more fine-tuning and reverse reinforcement learning.&#x20;
* **More Engaging Game Hub**: offering higher quality AI games with  more immersive experiences.

As our ecosystem has better AGI, better games, and more players,&#x20;

* **Players** can engage in hundreds of AI games across genres, enjoying diversified and reimagined experiences as well as fostering their digital identities, AI assets, and social networks.    &#x20;
* **Developers** can build quality AI games seamlessly and affordably and monetize from the largest AI player community via diversified methods of player top-ups, tokens, game NFTs, and AI assets.  &#x20;
